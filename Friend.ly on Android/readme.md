## Friend.ly on Android
 We’re really excited to share that we’re in the process of developing the Android version of Friend.ly! This update means you’ll soon be able to enjoy the full Friend.ly experience on your Android phone and tablet, with a design that’s smooth, fast, and optimized for the Android ecosystem. We’re focusing on a clean, responsive, and intuitive interface, built using Kotlin and Android Studio to ensure strong performance and a native Android feel.
